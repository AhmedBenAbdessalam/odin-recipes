Almond Tofu

Description

A dessert made out of almond. It has a silky-smooth texture with a long-lasting aroma of almond. It's named tofu only because of its tofu-like shape.

Ingredients

  • 1 Litre Almond Milk
  • 12 g Agar Powder

Optional

  • Osmanthus syrup to taste
  • Gojiberries garnish

Steps

  1. Add in agar powder and heat for 5 minutes.
  2. Pour almond pudding mixture into a container, straining through a mesh strainer once more. Remove bubbles on the top using a spoon.
  3. Cool and refrigerate until it sets. (Several hours.)
  4. When serving, pair with fruits or osmanthus syrup.
